腓立比書 4：6，7
甚麽事都不要憂慮，只要凡事藉着禱告和懇切祈求，連同感謝，把你們所請求的告訴上帝。
這樣，上帝就必賜你們平安，超越人所能理解的。
這種平安藉着基督耶穌，可以守護你們的內心和頭腦。 (Philippians 4:6，7)
Do not be anxious over anything，
but in everything by prayer and supplication along with thanksgiving let YOUR petitions be made known to God;
and the peace of God that excels all thought will guard YOUR hearts and YOUR mental powers by means of Christ Jesus.
